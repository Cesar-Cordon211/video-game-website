Open World Games

O

pen world games allow the player to fully explore the world, without a clear objective or purpose. The constant joy of finding something new or cool causes the player to explore more in hopes of finding something else. Most of these games don't explain how to play it, making it challenging for the inexperienced.

My Recommendations

Elden Ring

Elden Ring is a game that puts the player in a magical world called The Lands Between. The game came out in 2022. The game is all about combat in which the player has to find better equipment including weapons, armor and even spells.

The player can also customize their characters look, in which players create their personalized characters. This allows for players to create builds that go with their play style.

Minecraft

Minecraft was released in 2011, it has received many updates since then and is still a popular game to this day. The game has no clear objective, the player has to survive against different monsters that come out during the night.

The player can build anything they want, homes, monuments, art and even allows players to create complex machines with a material that works as electricity.
